About the Project

Nestland Farmhouse Plots are nature-first residential community developed with the intention of giving families an eco-conscious weekend escape near Pune. Overlooking the beautiful scenery, the project is designed to reconnect you with the outdoors.

With ready infrastructure, clear titles, and tar road access, we ensure a smooth investment process. Whether you dream of a quiet second home, a farmhouse, or an agro-tourism project, our location and planning make it all possible.

Our mission is to create peaceful, scenic spaces for modern families seeking balance, wellness, and natural beauty—without compromising on accessibility.

Why Choose Nestland Farmhouse Plots ?

  • ✅ Scenic Beauty All Around – Panoramic views of lush green hills, sparkling backwaters.
  • ✅ Year-Round Accessibility – Tar road touch plots – accessible even during monsoons.
  • ✅ Infrastructure Ready – Water & electricity connections available – start building immediately.
  • ✅ Unmatched Serenity – Clean air, peace, mild climate & excellent road connectivity to Pune.
  • ✅ Strong future appreciation potential – Pune’s fast-growing farmhouse belt.
  • ✅ Tax Benefits.
